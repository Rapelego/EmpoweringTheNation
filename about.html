About Empowering the Nation

Empowering Communities Through Skills

Founded by Precious Radebe in 2022, Empowering the Nation was created to help domestic workers and gardeners gain valuable professional and personal skills. Precious was inspired by her parents and community members who never had the chance to upskill themselves.

Our programmes are designed to empower learners to be more marketable, increase their earning potential, and even start their own small businesses. Through our courses, hundreds of individuals have already transformed their lives and careers.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower domestic workers and gardeners with accessible, practical, and life-changing training that promotes independence, confidence, and professional growth.

Our Vision

To create a future where every South African worker has the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to achieve financial stability and self-sufficiency.

Our Values

Integrity, empowerment, inclusivity, and lifelong learning are at the heart of everything we do.
